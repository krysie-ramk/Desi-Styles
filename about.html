About Us

Desi Styles' Story

Desi Styles is a home-grown fashion brand, started by Mrs. Krystal Ramkhelawan in 2008. Since then, it has become synonymous with indian ethnic wear that is fashionable, affordable and trendy in Trinidad and Tobago. We are located at #LP6.8 Clarke Drive, San Fernando and our operating hours are:


  • Monday - Friday : 9:00 am - 4:00 pm
  • Saturday : 10:00 am - 5:00 pm
  • Sunday: Closed
We also offer private appointments for bridal parties once contacted prior.

What can you do with a dream in your heart and an unshakable spirit of determination?

That is exactly what Mrs. Krystal Ramkhelawan started out with, along with a humble sum of TT$6,000 when she set out to create the iconic brand Desi Styles. The brand’s signature designs combined with the intricate detailing of hand block prints made people sit up and take note right away. With love pouring in from many local celebrities as well, Desi Styles became a household name, with retail stores cropping up across Trinidad to cater to the growing demand.



Today Desi Styles is…

  • BOLD
  • FEARLESS
  • TREND-SETTING
  • STYLISH
  • EFFORTLESS
  • INTRICATE
  • MODERN
  • AFFORDABLE

From using clean comfortable silhouettes to champion the modern Indian woman and man, to creating gorgeous designs steeped in our tradition, as well as presenting a line of chic and adorable clothing for children from 2 to 15 years. Today Desi Styles is a powerhouse of contemporary as well as Indian fashion. Our journey so far has been nothing but momentous and incredible, and the story behind the brand is as awe-inspiring as the woman who created it.
